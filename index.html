My Trip to the Great Smoky Mountains

One of my favorite vacations was visiting the Great Smoky Mountains National Park. There was a lot of fun things to do as well as some fantastic scenery.

Cade's Cove

Cade's Cove is a settlement in the national park. When the park was established, there were still residents living there.

As resident's passed away or left, no further resident's were allowed in, and the settlement was preserved as it used to be.

Hiking

Hiking in the park is a great activiy to do. There is a lot of spectacular scenery.

My favorite hiking spots were the many waterfalls in the park.

Whitewater Rafting

My favorite activiy was whitewater rafting.
